# Business Logic Guide - Semantic Layer Service

This document explains the business logic used to generate the Gold Layer (Business Intelligence Marts) from the Silver Layer (Canonical Data).

## 1. Top Customers
**Business Question**: "Who are our most valuable accounts?"
**Logic**: 
- Accounts are ranked globally based on their **Total Deal Value** (the sum of all deals, whether Won, Lost, or Open).
- The ranking uses a `ROW_NUMBER()` over the `total_deal_value` in descending order.
- **Strictness**: Accounts with missing names are excluded from this calculation.

## 2. Revenue Summary & Pipeline Value
**Business Question**: "What is our current financial exposure?"
**Logic**:
- **Won Value**: Sum of `deal_value` where the `deal_stage` is exactly 'Closed Won'.
- **Pipeline Value**: Sum of `deal_value` where the `deal_stage` DOES NOT start with 'Closed'. This represents active opportunities.
- **Average Deal Size**: The mean value of all deals associated with an account.
- **Strictness**: Only deals with valid numeric `deal_value` strings are summed (invalid strings are treated as 0).

## 3. Pipeline Health (Funnel)
**Business Question**: "How many deals are in each stage of our sales funnel?"
**Logic**:
- Aggregates all deals by their `deal_stage`.
- Only includes "Active" deals (stages that do not start with 'Closed').
- **Strictness**: Deals with NULL or empty stages are filtered out to prevent "Unknown" groupings in the UI.

## 4. At-Risk Accounts
**Business Question**: "Which customers are we losing touch with?"
**Logic**:
- **Risk Level Calculation**:
    - **High**: No recorded interactions OR last contact was > 60 days ago (and they have open deals).
    - **Medium**: Last contact was between 30 and 60 days ago (and they have open deals).
    - **Low**: Contacted within the last 30 days.
- Only accounts with at least one **Open Deal** are considered for risk analysis.

## 5. Account 360
**Business Question**: "Give me everything we know about this account."
**Logic**:
- This is a "Wide Table" join that merges:
    - Basic info (Industry, Geography, Employees) from `dim_account`.
    - Engagement metrics from `fact_interactions` (total count, latest sentiment).
    - Intelligence metrics from `fact_insights` (number of AI-extracted insights).
    - Performance metrics from `fact_deals` (win rate, pipeline value).

## 6. Activity & Engagement
**Business Question**: "How engaged are we with our customers?"
**Logic**:
- **Sentiment Score**: Calculated by assigning +1 to 'Positive' interactions, -1 to 'Negative', and 0 to others, then averaging.
- **Competitive Flags**: Counts insights where the AI detected competitor mentions or market threats.

---
**Data Quality Note**: The Gold Layer is designed to be "Strict". If a record is missing a primary identifier (like `account_name` or `deal_stage`), it is excluded from these reports to ensure that dashboard visualizations remain accurate and free of "Unknown" or "Null" categories.
